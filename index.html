⚓ Shifting Seas

Battleship, but the ships won’t sit still.

How to play

  • Fleet: Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (2), Scout (1) on a 10×10 grid.
  • Each turn: first you may move one ship 1 square, then fire one shot — or spend the shot on a power.
  • Movement: ships of size 2+ slide only forward or backward along their length. The 1-square Scout can step in any direction (never diagonally).
  • Rear hits: hit the tail of a ship and it’s immobilized — it can never move again. Damage travels with a ship when it moves; sink it by hitting every square.
  • 📡 UAV (every 5 turns): sweep any row or column — enemy-occupied squares flash red. Beware: ships may move after the sweep.
  • 💥 Triple shot (every 6 turns): fire 3 squares in a line at once.
  • Your old hits and misses stay pinned to the map — but the sea shifts. A "miss" square might hold a ship next turn…
